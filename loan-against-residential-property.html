Loan Against Residential Property Balance Transfer

A LAP Balance Transfer refers to the process of transferring your existing mortgage loan from the existing lender to your new lender. You can opt for the balance transfer of your property mortgage loan to Credify Capital.com for any of the following reasons:

  • Lower interest rate
  • Longer and more flexible loan tenure
  • Higher loan amount
  • Higher LTV on the market value of the asset
  • Best-in-class customer service and query resolution

How to Transfer Your Balance?

A representative from Credify Capital.com will get in touch with you for further processing. After your assessment and verification, you will receive a loan sanction. Once you authorize the disbursal, the loan amount will be credited to your bank account.
