  • Citizenship- The applicant should be an Indian citizen.
  • Employment- The applicant must have a full-time job at one of the following: Central Govt., PSU, MNC, Listed, Unlisted Public Ltd., Pvt. Ltd., LLP or companies that are listed internally.
